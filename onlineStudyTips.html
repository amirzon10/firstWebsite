TIPS FOR ONLINE LEARNING !

Online learning can present some unique challenges and it's common for students to struggle with time management and motivation to study.With the right preparation and study methods,online learning can be extremely rewarding and a great alternative to a traditional class setting.

  • A DEDICATED STUDY SPACE
    This has a lot of benefits as you can stay in your pj's all day. Choose a space where it's quiet and no other noises,so that you can focus on your work better.
  • TROUBLESHOOT ANY TECHNICAL ISSUES YOU ARE FACING
    This is an issue anyone can face,from unreliable internet connection to outdated software. The best ways to avoid them:
    1.Invest in a updated software laptop/computer.
    2.Buy a wifi signal booster.
    3.Using cloud-based backup for your important documents.
    4.Download necessary apps and elearning tools.
  • STAY ORGANISED AND BE THOROUGH
    Keep a semester and weekly calendar and make note of all the due dates, go through the syllabus,create, make to-do lists,digitize your notes
  • REMAIN ENGAGED THROUGHOUT THE COURSE
    Prepare and review every lesson,attend virtual classes and take notes,attempt all quizzes and tests and make a plan for improvement.
  • TAKE CARE OF YOURSELF
    Remember the goal you are working toward,stay healthy by getting adequate rest, nutrition and exercise,meditate and practice gratitude everyday
  • KNOW WHERE TO TURN FOR HELP
    Learn about the student services offered and know how to contact them. Don't be afraid to ask questions.
  • DON'T MULTITASK
    Stay focused on one thing at a time,you'll absorb more information and complete assignments with ease.
